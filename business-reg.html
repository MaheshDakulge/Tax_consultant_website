Business Registration & Incorporation

Seamless company formation — Private Limited, LLP, OPC, Partnership, and Proprietorship with end-to-end compliance support.

Reg.

Start Your Business the Right Way

Choosing the right business structure is one of the most important decisions an entrepreneur makes. The entity type affects your tax liability, compliance burden, fundraising ability, and personal liability. Our CAs guide you through the selection process and handle all MCA and Registrar of Companies (ROC) filings for a smooth, error-free incorporation.

What We Deliver

  • Private Limited Company incorporation (MCA-21 SPICe+ form)
  • Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) registration
  • One Person Company (OPC) formation
  • Partnership Firm deed drafting and registration
  • Sole Proprietorship establishment and registrations
  • DIN, DSC, and DPIN procurement
  • PAN, TAN, GST registration for new entities
  • MOA, AOA, and LLP agreement drafting
  • Annual ROC filing compliance (AOC-4, MGT-7)
  • Company name change, object change, and alteration procedures

Our Process

Step 01

Structure Advisory

We analyze your business goals, investor plans, and team size to recommend the optimal entity structure.

Step 02

Documentation & Filing

We prepare all required documents and file with MCA/ROC for incorporation, typically within 5–7 working days.

Step 03

Post-Incorporation Setup

Bank account opening, registrations (GST, PAN, TAN), and initial compliance calendar setup.

Why Register Your Business Properly

Limited Liability

Protect your personal assets from business debts and legal claims.

Credibility

Registered entities are trusted by banks, clients, and investors.

Investment Ready

Private Limited companies can raise equity capital and issue shares.

Frequently Asked Questions

A Private Limited Company is better for startups seeking investment, requiring equity sharing, or planning to scale significantly. An LLP is ideal for professional services, partnerships seeking operational flexibility, and lower compliance burden.

A Private Limited Company can typically be incorporated in 7–10 working days from document submission. LLP registration takes 5–7 days. We handle all MCA filings to ensure the fastest possible turnaround.
